Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 May 2026

Overview

The Architect’s Loop provides browser-based utility tools at thearchitectsloop.com. The current tools are designed to calculate or generate reports within your browser without submitting the values you enter to a The Architect’s Loop database.

Information entered into the tools

Inputs entered into the Ad Revenue Potential Estimator and Founder Website Visibility Report are processed in your browser for the purpose of generating on-screen results. The current site does not include an account system or database that stores those inputs.

Third-party services

The website loads Tailwind CSS from a third-party content delivery network for page styling. When your browser retrieves that resource, the third-party provider may receive ordinary technical request information such as IP address, browser data and requested URL.

The optional PayPal support link takes you to PayPal when clicked. Any information you provide there is governed by PayPal’s own policies, not this website.

If you contact us by email, your email message and address will be received and stored through our email provider for the purpose of responding to your message.

Analytics and advertising

The current public pages do not include analytics scripts or programmatic advertising code. If analytics or advertising technologies are introduced later, this policy will be updated before or alongside that change.
