Legal / Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 3 June 2026

Overview

The Architect’s Loop provides browser-based utility tools at thearchitectsloop.com. The toolkit is designed so calculations, conversions, previews, and file handling run in your browser. The tools do not use a The Architect’s Loop account system or database for the values and files you enter.

Tool processing

Inputs entered into public tools (including estimators, visibility reports, Markdown conversion, schema generators, CSV workbench, SEO previews, and similar pages) are processed locally in your browser to produce on-screen results, copyable code, or downloadable files. Those inputs are not sent to a The Architect’s Loop server for tool processing.

Optional homepage email signup

The homepage offers an optional “Notify Me” form. If you choose to submit it, only your email address is sent to Kabak.info (form endpoint tal-notify-me) so we can notify you about new tools. You do not need to sign up to use any tool. Kabak’s own privacy terms apply to that service.

Files and CSV data

Tools such as the Markdown converter and CSV Workbench let you choose files from your device. Those files are read locally in your browser with the File API. They are not uploaded to The Architect’s Loop for processing. Do not use highly sensitive data unless you accept processing on your device.

Third-party services

Most pages load Tailwind CSS from a third-party CDN for styling. The Obsidian & Notes Toolkit may load Mermaid from a CDN to preview diagrams. When your browser requests those resources, the provider may receive ordinary technical data (such as IP address, browser type, and requested URL).

The optional Payhip download library link takes you to Payhip when clicked. Any information you provide there is governed by PayPal/Payhip policies.

If you contact us by email, your message is handled through our email provider for the purpose of responding to you.

Analytics, advertising, and UTM links

The public toolkit pages do not include analytics scripts or programmatic advertising code maintained by The Architect’s Loop. The UTM Link Builder only creates URLs you can copy; it does not install tracking on your site. If you add analytics on your own website, that is separate from these tools.

Contact

Questions about this policy may be sent to thearchitectsloop@proton.me.
