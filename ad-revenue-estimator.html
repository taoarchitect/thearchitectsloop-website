    How This Tool Works

    Estimate website ad revenue potential

    What it models

    Enter pageviews, property type, audience geography, mobile share and ad placement density to view low, realistic and high indicative monthly revenue scenarios.

    What RPM means

    RPM represents estimated revenue per 1,000 views. This calculator blends indicative RPM ranges by audience tier and applies the selected device and density assumptions.

    Important limitation

    The result is not guaranteed income. Real advertising revenue varies with ad network, content, seasonality, fill rate, viewability, user behaviour and advertiser demand.

    Frequently asked questions

    Is this an AdSense earnings guarantee?

    No. It is a planning estimator based on the assumptions you enter, not a prediction or approval guarantee from any advertising network.

    Does this tool store my inputs?

    No database submission is used by this tool. Calculations run in your browser.
