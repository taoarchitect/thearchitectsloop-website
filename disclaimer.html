The Architect’s Loop tools provide informational estimates and structured planning outputs. They are not professional financial, advertising, legal, accounting or search-engine-optimization advice.

Ad Revenue Potential Estimator

Revenue calculations are scenario estimates based on the values and assumptions entered into the tool. Actual advertising income can differ materially because of traffic quality, audience location, seasonality, ad-blocking, viewability, advertiser demand, ad network rules, approval requirements and other conditions outside this tool.

Founder Website Visibility Report

This report is a manual readiness checklist based on information supplied by the user. It does not crawl a website, perform live technical tests, submit pages to search engines or guarantee indexing, impressions, search position, traffic or sales.

Markdown to HTML & PDF Converter

Markdown conversion output depends on the structure of the Markdown supplied by the user. The tool supports common Markdown patterns, but it is not a full publishing platform, legal document formatter, accessibility auditor, or exact clone of Obsidian’s graph view. Users should review exported HTML, text, and print/PDF output before publication or use.
