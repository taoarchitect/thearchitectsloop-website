General information only

The Architect’s Loop tools provide informational estimates, previews, generated code, and structured planning outputs. They are not professional financial, advertising, legal, tax, or search-engine-optimization advice.

Revenue and business math tools

The Ad Revenue Potential Estimator and Founder Business Math toolkit (pricing, break-even, profit margin, and revenue scenarios) produce scenario estimates from the values you enter. Actual results can differ because of market conditions, costs, traffic quality, seasonality, and other factors.

Website readiness and SEO tools

The Founder Website Visibility Report is a manual checklist. Robots.txt Builder, Sitemap.xml Builder, SEO Snippet Preview, Social Preview Generator, and UTM Link Builder produce files or previews for planning. They do not crawl your live site, guarantee rankings, or ensure search engines will display results exactly as previewed.

Publishing, schema, and data tools

Markdown conversion, frontmatter, schema generators, Markdown tables, CSV Workbench, and Obsidian toolkit outputs depend on the content you supply. Validate HTML, JSON-LD, robots.txt, sitemap.xml, and CSV exports before production use. Structured data should be tested with appropriate validator tools.

Use of outputs

Users should verify important decisions using their own data and, when appropriate, qualified professional guidance. Use of these tools and their outputs is at the user’s discretion.
