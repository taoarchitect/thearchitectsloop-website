          About This Report

          A manual website SEO readiness checklist for founders

          What it reviews

          Record your title, description, headline, audience, technical checks and trust signals to generate a structured visibility-readiness report and action plan.

          Who it helps

          This checklist is designed for founders, freelancers and small website owners reviewing a landing page or preparing a new website for launch.

          Important limitation

          This is not an automated crawler, performance test or live ranking checker. It generates guidance from the information and checkboxes you enter.

          Frequently asked questions

          Will this guarantee Google rankings?

          No. The report helps identify basic readiness gaps; it does not guarantee indexing, traffic or ranking outcomes.

          Does this tool crawl or store my website details?

          No. It is a manual browser-based report generator and does not submit your entered inputs to a database.
